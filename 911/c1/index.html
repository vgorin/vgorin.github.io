Three Steps to Start Playing

Back to TOC / Back to TOP

Only three quick steps to start playing right now!

  1. Register (create your ID and PWD to login into game)
  2. Download game client and patch, unpack to your desired client folder
  3. Run system\l2.exe from your client folder, login with your registered ID and PWD
